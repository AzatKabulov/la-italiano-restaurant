La Nostra Storia

A family tradition
since 1987

"Good food is the foundation of genuine happiness." — Every dish at La Italiano is made as if your grandmother were watching.

Chef Marco Tessore brought his family's recipes from Palermo. We use no semi-finished products — every sheet of pasta dough is rolled by hand each morning. Our wood-fired oven was shipped directly from Sicily.
